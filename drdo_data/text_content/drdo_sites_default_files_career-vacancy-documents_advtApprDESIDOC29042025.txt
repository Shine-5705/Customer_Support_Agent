2.  Applications  duly  filled  by  the  candidates  should  reach  at  the  following  address:-  The  Director, 

DESIDOC, Metcalfe House, Delhi-110 054.  

3.  The selected apprentices against the above-noted vacancies will be posted either to DESIDOC, Delhi-

110054 or DP&C, DRDO HQ, DRDO Bhawan, New Delhi-110011. 

4.  The candidates claiming reservation under SC/ST/OBC category have to submit the certificate as per 
central government format, failing which their claim for reservation will be considered as ‘General’ 
category only. 

5.  UPPER  AGE  Limit:  Candidates  should  be  maximum  of 28  years age  on  date  of  written/interview 

(relaxable by five years for SC/ST and three years for OBC candidates). 

6.  Last date to submit applications: 21 days from the date of publication on DRDO website.   
7.  Selection Procedure and Criteria: Candidates will be selected on written/interview basis after short 
listing on the merit basis (percentage/marks of essential qualification).The mode of selection may 
change as per the requirements of organization.  

8.  Shortlisted candidates must bring all original relevant certificates at the time of verification/interview. 
9.  The board will be constituted by Director, DESIDOC for scrutiny of the applications and to shortlist 

the candidates (as per the vacancies indicated above) as per selection criteria. 

10.  Only selected candidates will be informed through offer letter/Email. 
11.  Enrollment  under  Apprentice  Act  1961:  It  is  mandatory  for  candidates  to  enroll  themselves  on 
National  Apprenticeship  Training  Scheme  (NATS)  portal  (http://www.mhrdnats.gov.in)  or 
(http://nats.education.gov.in) before applying for apprenticeship training. 

12.  Candidates who have already undergone apprenticeship training in any institute/organization are not 
eligible. The candidates who have passed in 2022, 2023 and 2024 are only eligible. A certificate in 
this regard may be furnished.  

13.  It may be explicitly understood that subsequent to the  apprenticeship period, DRDO shall not offer  

absorption/any type of employment in DRDO. 

14.  No TA/DA will be paid to the applicants called at any stage of the selection process.

5.  Graduate degree holders, who had training or job experience for a period of one year or more 
after  attaining  the  essential  qualification,  shall  NOT  be  eligible  for  being  engaged  as  an 
apprentice under the Act. 

6.  The  period  of  training  will  be  12  months  commencing  from  execution  of  the  contract  of 
Apprenticeship. Candidates who have been terminated from apprenticeship training from any 
organization / institute are not eligible to apply. 

7.  Reservation  will  be  applied  as  per  the  provisions  of  the  Apprenticeship  Act,  1961  and 
Apprenticeship  Rules  1992  as  amended  from  time  to  time.  Applicants  belonging  to  SC 
(Scheduled Caste/ST (Scheduled Tribes)/OBC Other Backward Classes) – Non-Creamy layer 
shall produce their caste certificates in prescribed format issued by the competent authority. 

8.  Selected candidates have to submit the ‘Medical Fitness Certificate’ at the time of joining. 

9.  No TA/DA will be paid to the applicants called at any stage of the selection process. 

10.  The  selected  candidates  must  execute  a  contract  for a  period  of  one  year  of  training  as  per 

provision of the Apprenticeship Act, 1961 and rules framed there under. 

11.  Before applying, the candidates should ensure that he/she fulfils the eligibility criteria and other 

norms mentioned in the advertisement. 

12.  There is no provision for appointment of the apprentice trainee in DESIDOC or DRDO after 

the completion of training and candidate cannot claim for the same. 

13.  Suppression of facts will lead to disqualification at any stage of the selection process. 

14.  DESIDOC  reserves  the  right  to  increase/decrease  the  number  of  seats  for  apprenticeship 

training. 

15.  Any canvassing or personal follow up with an intention for influencing the process of selection 
of apprentices by and on behalf of any of the candidates shall lead to immediate cancellation of 
their applications. 

16.  DESIDOC  reserves  the  right  to  withdraw/cancel  the  advertisement/selection  process  if 

circumstances so warrant without assigning any reason thereof. 

17.  In case of any dispute, the legal jurisdiction shall be that of the Hon’ble High Court of Delhi.  

18.  The  apprentices  will  not  be  provided  any  quarters/hostel  accommodations/transport  by 

DESIDOC/DRDO establishment during the training period.
