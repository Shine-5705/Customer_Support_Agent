155mm X 52 Cal Gun Barrel with 23 litres Chamber Capacity 

During  the  design  and  development  of  weapon  system  (Barrel,  Breech 
Mechanism, Muzzle Brake & Recoil System), ARDE designed & developed 155 mm 
X 52 Cal Barrel with 23 litres chamber capacity.  

155 mm x 52 Cal gun barrel with 23 litres chamber capacity is suitable for firing up to 
Zone-6  charge  and  achieve  corresponding  range  requirements,  with  In-service 
155mm ammunition. The 155mm x 52 Cal 23 litres Chamber capacity barrel is well 
proven  and  exploited  sufficiently  to  check  its  design  ruggedness.  The  salient 
parameters of the 155 mm x 52 Cal gun barrel with 23 litres chamber capacity are as 
follows: 

a)  Bore Dimension: 154.94+0.05 mm 
b)  Barrel Length: 8060 mm 
c)  Bore Profile: Rifled (Right Hand) 
d)  No of grooves: 48 
e)  Rate of rifling: 1 Turn in 20 Calibre 
f)  Barrel Weight: 2032 kg (Approx.) 
g)  Chamber Volume: 23 litres 
h)  Manufacturing Process: Partial Length Hydraulic Autofrettaged 
i)  Compatibility:  

a.  All types of charges (Bags/ BMCS) 
b.  All 155 mm In-service Shells.   

j)  Material: ESR Steel
