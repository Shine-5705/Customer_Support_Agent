CBRN RESUSCITATOR (MANUAL)

CBRN Resuscitator (Manual) is used for the first aid treatment of soldiers with respiratory failure in

case   of   chemical   warfare   (CW)   attack.   It   is   especially   designed   for   emergency   ventilation   and

resuscitation.  It comprises of a facemask, a canister for purifying the contaminated air, a three-

layered activated charcoal/ ACS based cloth to protect the exposed face portion from CW agents as

well as to firmly anchor the face mask onto the wearer and a non-breathing valve system. The set is

handy, durable, compact and  capable of delivering approximately one litre tidal volume.  The item

has been accepted by the Users.

Salient features:

• Portable manual resuscitation system designed and tested specifically for use in chemical and

biological conditions.

• Made of bromobutyl rubber and a gas filter adapter, which is ideal for manual resuscitation in

toxic atmospheres such as in fire and chemical accidents.  

• Highly   flexible   butyl   rubber   extension   tube   offering   unparalleled   flexibility   during   casualty

transport.
