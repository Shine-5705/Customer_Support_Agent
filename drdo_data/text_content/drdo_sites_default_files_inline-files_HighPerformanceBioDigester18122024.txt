High Performance Biodigester (HPBD) Technology 

Defence Research & Development Establishment (DRDE), Gwalior, has developed a 

High Performance Biodigester (HPBD) Technology comprising of anaerobic as well as 

aerobic digestion system for human waste treatment. This technology can significantly 

improve sanitation status in India and other countries. The HPBD is best suitable for use

in rural communities not having access to sewer system (non-sewered system) and in 

urban areas pursuing sustainable sanitation solutions. HPBD may also be used for 

temporary settlements or congregations. The HPBD meets NGT norms recommended 

for recycling and reuse of treated wastewater for Landscaping Horticulture & Agriculture

as referred in Govt of India, Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs letter no D. O Q-

12011/9/2023-CPHEEO dated 18/12/2023.
