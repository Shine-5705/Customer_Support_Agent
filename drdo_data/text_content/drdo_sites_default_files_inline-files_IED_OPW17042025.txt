Improvised Explosive Device (IED) Containment Vessel

A one-time use, cost-effective, lightweight IED containment vessel has been  developed at R&DE(E), Pune
for safe detonation of explosives by law-enforcement agencies in crowded public areas. The containment
vessel can successfully detonate a 6 kg IED within it. The vessel is carried on a crane mounted trolley for
easy transportation and deployment.

Salient Features

 Contain up to 6 kg IED

 Unique sandwich construction

Technical Specification

 Weight 350 kg

 1 m diameter

 1 m height
