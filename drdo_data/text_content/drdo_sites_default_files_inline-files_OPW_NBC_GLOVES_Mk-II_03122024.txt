NBC GLOVES Mk-II

NBC Gloves Mk-II is an integral part of Individual Protective Equipment (IPE) used
in the nuclear, biological and chemical warfare environment and provides passive
protection to the hands of the wearer. The gloves comply with the requirements of
JSQR 8415-002. NBC Gloves Mk-II is compatible to the NBC Suit Mk-V and other
IPE’s. The gloves are designed to fit four Indian sizes namely Small (S), Medium
(M)   Large   (L)   &   Extra-large   (XL).   The   gloves   are   to   be   used   along   with   the
white/off-white   inner   gloves   of   knitted,   blend   of   cotton/viscose   or   lycra   or   their
combinations in three sizes to match the outer gloves.
Salient features:

 Weight of one pair (XL) less than 300 g (including inner liner gloves)

 Protection from chemical Warfare Agent (HD) –BTT more than 24 hrs

 Gloves are Ambidextrous 

 Withstand the environmental conditions as 810F  JSQR

 Dimensions referred  as per JSS 8415-16 (2016) for NBC Butyl Gloves

 Leak proof, water proof, antistatic & flame retardant

 Can be decontaminated & destroyed

 Provision of Inner stretchable fabric gloves for comfort & sweat absorption

 Shelf life: minimum 10 years (RH 65±5%; Temperature 27±5˚C)
