Introduction:

Military Combat Parachute System (MCPS) is a mode of insertion used by SF to penetrate
paratroopers with combat load into designated area of operations.   MCPS allows Paratroopers to
jump from an aircraft and deploy their Parachutes at pre-determined Altitude, Navigate and Land
Safely. MCPS system can be used in High Altitude High Opening (HAHO), High Altitude Medium
Opening   (HAMO)   &   High-Altitude   Low   Opening   (HALO)   modes   as   required   for   the   particular
scenario. The parachute system has been designed as per international standard and qualification
test   schedule   (QTS)   has   been   prepared   in   line   with   Parachute   Industry   Association   (PIA)   and
approved by Centre for Military Airworthiness and Certification (CEMILAC).
