Pilot Parachute, BMK-41 Assembly, Part No. ADRDE/1164 

Pilot Parachute, BMK-41 Assembly, Part No.ADRDE/1164 is a lifesaving equipment.  It 
is  installed  on  the  ejection  seat.  During  emergency/malfunctioning  of  aircraft,  pilot 
parachute brings down the pilot safely on ground. Pilot parachute mainly consists of first 
stage  drogue  parachute,  second  stage  drogue  parachute,  main  canopy  and  harness.  Pilot 
parachute is fabricated using various textile materials and metal parts. The textile materials 
consist  of  fabrics,  narrow  fabrics  (tapes  and  webbings),  cordages.  Majority  of  textile 
materials are made of Nylon.
