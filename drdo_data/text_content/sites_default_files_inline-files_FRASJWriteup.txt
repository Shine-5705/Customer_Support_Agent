FLAME RETARDANT AIRCREW SURVIVAL JACKET 

The Aircrew Survival Jacket (ASJ) is intended to house the Pilot location beacon (PLB) 

assembly including V/UHF whip antenna and GPS. Airworthiness obtained from RCMA 

(AC). 

Salient features: 

•  The PLB along with accessories helps to identify the geographical location of the 

pilot who has ejected out of the aircraft. 

•  Made out of combination of flame-retardant material woven and knitted fabric 

•  Withstand wind blast pressure of 600 KEAS 

•  Various versions developed for KIRAN, MIG, Mirage & Jaguar series
