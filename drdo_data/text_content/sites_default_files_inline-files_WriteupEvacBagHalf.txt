Bag Evacuation Casualty (Half) (Positive Pressure Bag Equipped with PAPR) 

Positive  pressure  evacuation  bag  (Half)  is  used  by  an  individual  when  the  affected 
Casualty is able to walk on his own without the assistance of attendant or Stretcher rods or the 
Lumbar pad.   The Positive pressure Evacuation Bag with Power Air purified Respirator (PAPR) 
is available in 03 different sizes.   

Salient Features: 

•  Bag can quickly inflated and provide air-tight protection against contaminants.  

•  Design allows easy entry of the casualty into the Bag,  

•  Equipped with high performance PAPR and provided with filters with change over option 

of filters. 

•  The blower is AC/DC operatable. Status of the Battery can be observed. 

•  Equipped with Medical Ports for safe entry of intravenous lines or oxygen tubing/cables. 

• 

Impermeable film is provided on the upper surface to  overcome claustrophobia effect. 

•  Equipped with air and water tight zippers, Composite stretcher Rods, Lumbar pad
